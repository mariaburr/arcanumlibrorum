Never had a good Christmas all my life, nor a good new year, and now... I am very bitter but fortunately not revengeful, though I feel should be how I am... The scars on her back never faded as long as she lived, lacerated as she was. As she approached, trembling, he held out a branch of the most beautiful leaves and magnificent flowers, saying, "Take these, beloved, and offer them to the lady." He pointed her out, sitting like an empress on a dais near the altar. She held out the branch she had received. The lady took it, broke off a sprig, and gave it back, saying, "take special care of this for me."
