Extinguished candlelight lingers in the shadow. There be divers sorts of death: some wherein the body remains, and in others it passes quite away with the spirit. Another disturbance. You remember being here? They were startled by the appearance. The anguished man rushed into the room. No more sign of her there than if never born. Consternation prevailed. Great search made, but no one to be found. After night and day in misery, the poor bridegroom laid down to rest. In a while he seemed to wake from a troubled dream. The moon shone in the window. In the slanting rays she stood in white. His tongue was without utterance, and limbs unable to move.
