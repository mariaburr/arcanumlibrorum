Ash borrowings continued the pain I long for replaced the end of days for an hour ligatures impressed upon the soft skin of women imprisoned in the arms of men seized her by her hair beat her till she tired of it mocked by revellers they hold me up to ridicule I cannot restrain my sobbing Do not be afraid, go now, I will deliver you into the brighteness of day. We have laboured in vain. I can see two apparitions dressed in white who accompany the maiden always and defend her from all assaults. The mightiest men have hitherto bowed with reverence before the saint. Why did they thus bow?
