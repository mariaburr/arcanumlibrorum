A series of attempted escapes ended successfully.
His dress was shabby, though he attempted to keep his clothes clean and mended,
bereft through death or disappearance, abandonment or desertion, or separation or loss
largely solitary
after his father became crippled

did not discover the whereabouts
of tranquil flowered landscapes populated by children and fantastic creatures
originated as a collection of settlements on the outskirts

dedicated every remaining day to composing
a single-spaced fantasy manuscript
whose endless pages concluded: I had a very poor nothing like Christmas. Never had a good Christmas all my life, nor a good new year, and now... I am very bitter but fortunately not revengeful, though I feel should be how I am...
